Share with other organisations

Add a link to your website

If you add a link to our website, you help us show up higher in search engines. This makes it easier for activists to find our training resources on Google.

Either share a link to our homepage, or link to an article that you find interesting (even better!): https://activisthandbook.org/

Social media

If you share our platform on your Insta story, Twitter or Facebook, it helps us a lot! Make it personal, add a photo of yourself (at a protest for example)!

With a photo, you will reach way more people. And did you know search engines like Google also give a higher search rank to websites that are shared on socials?

Your role

Summary: As facilitator of the translation sub-circle you will build and support a diverse group of translators from across the world. You will work closely together with language and regional represesntatives to support and engage people that want to localise the content of Activst Handbook. You will also be responsible for developing a localisation workflow that suits the capacity and needs of the translators.

Role components: Find the exact purpose, domain and accountabilities of this role in Airtable.

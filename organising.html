Capacity building

You want to make a big impact. But you have a limited access to resources needed to get there. This section focusses on building capacity to bring about change.

Learn how to increase the number of people in your organisation, through activating supporters (mobilisation), activating non-supporters (recruitment), and keeping your activists active (engagement).

Learn how to grow knowledge in your members (training), grow the knowledge in your organisation (organisational learning), and manage that knowledge in a systemic way (knowledge management).

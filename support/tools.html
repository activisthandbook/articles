In this article, we explain what digital tools our volunteers use frequently. We highly encourage you to ask for help if you have trouble using one of these tools.

Need some help? Send a message to our tech support: tech@activisthandbook.org

Google Workplace

Every Activist Handbook volunteer gets their own personal Google Workspace account. This includes:

  • Personal email address (name@activisthandbook.org)
  • 30GB Drive storage
  • Google apps (Calendar, Meet and more)

Ask a coordinator to get access to your Google Workspace account. 

For security reasons, we strongly recommend to turn on 2-step verification immediately after creating your account!

Airtable

Use this invite link to join our workspace (only accessible with your Google Workspace account).

Slack

We use Slack to communicate. Make sure to download the app and turn on notifications. You can either join our Slack by signing in with your Google Workspace account or with this invite link.

Website

Sign in using your Google Workspace account by clicking the ‘Volunteers’ signup method.

Grammarly

Volunteers get free access to Grammarly Pro. Ask a coordinator to give you your signing details.

Google Drive

We save all our files in our Google Drive folder. Need editing access? Feel free to ask our tech support!

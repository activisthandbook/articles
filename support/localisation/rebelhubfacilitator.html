Rebel Hub facilitator

Summary: As a rebel hub facilitator you are responsible for building and facilitating a rebel hub. A rebel hub is a network of local activists, movements and changemakers. In the process of organising the rebel assembly the role description of rebel hub facilitor will be developed further. This role will work closely together with the rebel hub content compiler, rebel hub event organiser and rerbel hub outreach

Role definition: You can find the exact purpose, domain and accountabilities of this role in Airtable.

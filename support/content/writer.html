In this guide, we will help you to get started as a writer. If you are already a writer, we encourage you to edit this guide to make it fit better with your working style. This will help both you and your successor!

Writing workflow

  1. Submit the topic idea via this form. The content coordinator will review your topic idea before the upcoming meeting
    • Topic title
    • Short description
    • Home chapter
    • Relevant chapters
  2. Join the first upcoming writers meeting to hear feedback and develop your topic idea. We can discuss matching you with other writers so you write as buddies and agree on reviewing each other's work.
  3. Meet weekly in one of two time slots with the coordinator and other writers to check in and either
    • Work independently with your buddy.
    • Get support from content coordinator where we will discuss coworking dynamics, and provide support related to writing
  4. When you finish your article, submit it for peer review. One of our other writers will read your article and give feedback.
  5. Publish the article on the website and send a social media request for promotion. If you need help publishing the article on our website, contact our tech coordinator.

Writer check-ins

Choose from one of these time slots to join the weekly check-ins (if none of these work, contact the content coordinator):

  • Timeslot 1: Sunday, 18:00 UTC (for North & South America, Europe and Africa)
  • Timeslot 2: Saturday, 10:00 UTC (for Europe, Middle East, Africa, Asia and Australia)

Reach out to our content coordinator to receive the link for the video call: contact@activisthandbook.org

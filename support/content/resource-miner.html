In this guide, we will help you to get started as a Resource miner. If you are already in this role, we encourage you to edit this guide to make it fit better with your working style. This will help both you and your successor! 

Your role

Summary: As resource miner your main purpose is to find resources on priority topics the Content circle is working on. Your goals are focused around external libraries, you will be updating our Resource page and providing teams of writers with resources on topics they are working on at any moment.

Role components: Find the exact purpose, domain and accountabilities of this role in Airtable.

Managing the resource library

We're aware that as the Activist Handbook grows, navigating the resource library is going to become difficult. There was a talk of using #tags in combination with pages on resource pages, but this idea is far from being actionable. We're hoping you can find a good solution.

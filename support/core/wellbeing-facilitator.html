In this guide, we will help you to get started as a wellbeing facilitator. We encourage you to to keep this document up-to-date. This will help you and your successor! Also make sure to check out the other guides for the core circle.

Your role

Summary: As a wellbeing facilitator, you are the first contact point for volunteers regarding their wellbeing. You will work closely together with your fellow wellbeing facilitator and the community builder.

Role definition: You can find the exact purpose, domain and accountabilities of this circle in Airtable.

Contributing to Activist Handbook should be easy. That is why we wrote some guides to help you get started. If you are a new member, this is the place to be. Before you continue, make sure to join our community.

We know that seeing all of these guides can be a bit overwhelming. At the very least, we recommend new volunteers to read our article on the basics. In addition, we always schedule a video call to help you get started.

Getting started

We highly recommend every new member to read these guides:

  • The basics: At the very least, you should read this guide.
  • Our organisation: We work with circles and roles. Both have clearly defined what their purpose, domain and accountabilities are. Read this article to find out more about who does what.
  • Our meetings: We meet on a regular basis. Read this guide to find out what kind of meetings we have.
  • Tools we use: All volunteers use Slack to communicate. Google Workspace is for our calendar, email, video calls and files. Read this guide to find out how to get started with these tools.
  • Using our website: Anyone can edit pages on our website. To learn more about how to use tags, translations, version history and more, read this article.
  • Using the logbook: Everything we do, we write down in our organisation logbook.
  • Accessibility: We try to make our organisation accessible to everyone. Read this article if you have trouble understanding English, have bad internet, or no access to a computer.
